About AgriAlert

AgriAlert supports farmers and advisers in Rwanda with timely weather information and a simple way to record farm and crop details for alert preferences.

What we offer

District-level current conditions, rainfall likelihood, and a five-day temperature outlook help you plan field work, irrigation, and harvest timing. Registration captures your district, main crop, and how you prefer to be reached so alerts can be matched to your operation.

How to use AgriAlert

  • On Home, enter your district name and review the forecast for the days ahead.
  • Complete Register with your name, phone or email, district, crop, and alert frequency.
  • Review your entries under My alerts whenever you need a record of what you submitted.

Who it is for

Smallholders, cooperatives, and extension staff who need a clear, quick view of weather risk alongside a structured way to sign up for advisory messages.
